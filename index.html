About

Max Velocity Sports Performance is a company who works with elite / professional athletes, teams and those looking to make a serious change to their health. We look at specific key physical indicators to take you to the next level of human performance and health. This is achieved by working with each individual client in a number of way by testing, assessing, movement screening and complete programming for our clients.
